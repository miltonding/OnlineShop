apache默认静态资源路径： E:\xampp\htdocs


1. 在hosts文件里给127.0.0.1添加
serverName  static.webapp.com


2. 修改httpd-vhosts.conf文件
<VirtualHost *:80>
	##ServerAdmin webmaster@dummy-host2.example.com
	
	##Access control for http://localhost:8080
	Header set Access-Control-Allow-Origin http://localhost:8080
	
	##Must in apache root htdocs
    DocumentRoot "E:/xampp/htdocs/static"
    ServerName static.webapp.com
    ErrorLog "E:/xampp/apache/logs/apache2.localhost-error.log"
    CustomLog "E:/xampp/apache/logs/apache2.localhost-access.log" common

	<Directory "E:/xampp/htdocs/static">
		AllowOverride All
		Require all granted
	</Directory>
</VirtualHost>
